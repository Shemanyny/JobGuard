Built to protect job seekers from scams

JobGuard helps you verify job postings before you apply by analyzing common scam patterns and trust signals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help job seekers avoid fake and misleading job opportunities by making verification simple, fast, and reliable, so they can apply with confidence.

Our Story

The idea behind JobGuard came from real experiences shared by job seekers who had been misled by fake job postings. Some wasted hours applying, others lost money, and many were left frustrated and discouraged. We realized that job seekers needed a simple way to check opportunities before committing their time or sharing personal details. JobGuard
was designed as a solution, a tool that scans job postings for red flags and provides clear insights so users can make safer, more informed decisions

What We Check

  • Company name legitimacy
  • Official company website
  • Domain age and credibility
  • Company online presence
  • Job description authenticity
  • Unrealistic salary claims
  • Vague or copied job content
  • Pressure or urgency language
  • Contact method legitimacy
  • Email domain authenticity
